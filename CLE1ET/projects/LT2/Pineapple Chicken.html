Pineapple Chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsp canola or sesame oil
  • 1 (10oz) package frozen broccoli (or stir-fry vegetable mix), thawed
  • 1/2 C stir-fry sauce
  • 1/4 C pinappel juice, use reserved juice from canned pinapple
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (optional - use if you like it hot)
  • 1 (15oz) canned pinapple chunks or tidbits, drained (reserve the juice!)
  • 2 C diced cooked chicken or 2 (10oz) cans chicken breast, drained and flaked

METHOD

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over a medium high heat. Add all ingredients
    EXCEPT the pinapple and chicken. Cook and stir until heated through, 5-6 mins.
  2. Add pineapple and chicken; cook another 2 minutes.
    Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta.
    *Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold
    running water for several minutes.
