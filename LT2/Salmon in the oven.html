Salmon in the oven

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 fillet of salmon, skinned (tails are cheapest)
  • Any veggies you like: tomato, peppers, zuchinnis, etc.
  • Liquid cream
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

METHOD

  1. Put the salmon in an oven dish - add chopped veggies and cream
    on top with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill on high heat for about 25 mins. If it is a thick fillet, it
    will take longer to cook through, always check if the middle
    is cooked.
  3. You can serve it with rice or pasta.
